Pizza

This easy but delicious pizza will have you coming back for more.

With just a few simple ingredients, you too can make this pizza.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp. active dry yeast
  • 1 tsp. sugar OR honey
  • 1 1/2 cups of warm water
  • 3 tbsp. olive oil
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups whole-wheat flour
  • 16 ounces mozzarella(grated)
  • 1-2 16 oz. jars of tomato sauce

Equipment

  • 2 large sheet pans
  • Measuring cups and spoons
  • Large bowl
  • Wooden spoon
  • Kitchen towel
  • Oven

Steps

  1. Prepare dough by adding the all-purpose flour, sugar and yeast to a large bowl and stir to combine
  2. Add the warm water and mix with a wooden spoon for 3-4 minutes.
  3. Mix in the salt and the whole wheat flour, one cup at a time. The dough should becoome too difficult to mix with the spoon.
  4. Dump the dough onto a clean, lightly floured surface. Knead it with your hands until it becomes smooth and does not stick to your fingers, about 10 minutes.
  5. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl and cover with a damp cloth. let the dough rise for 30-40 minutes.
  6. Once the dough has risen, preheat the oven to 425F. Divide dough in half and place each half onto a lightly oiled pan and spread out the edges with your hands to form a thin crust.
  7. Spread tomato sauce over the crust, leaving a 1-inch rim. Top with cheese and other toppings of your choice.
  8. Place the pizza in the preheated oven and bake for 15-20 minutes, or until crust is golden brown.
